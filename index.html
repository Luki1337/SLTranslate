Welcome to SignTranslate

Our Goal

Welcome to SignTranslate, a website that translates sign language into English from images or videos. Upload your media and our machine learning model provides instant translations. Perfect for learners, communicators, and the curious, SignTranslate makes sign language accessible to everyone.

How Our Model Works

Our model allows our site to recognize signs from images using algorithms trained on large datasets. When an image is uploaded, our model analyzes it, recognizes the signs, and provides a translation.
